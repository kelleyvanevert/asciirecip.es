  RUSSIAN TEACAKES
  ================

╭ * 225g (1 C) butter
│ * 1 tsp vanilla sugar or extract
│ * 6 TB powdered sugar              (+ more)
│
│ * 2 C flour
│ * 1 C finely chopped (wal)nuts
╰ * 1/4 tsp salt

➭ Knead well

➭ Roll into 1 inch balls,
   separate 2 inches from each other

➭ Bake @ 180ºC for about 10-12min,
   until lightly golden

➭ When cooled, roll in powdered sugar,
   and possibly repeat a second time
